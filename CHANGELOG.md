# Changelog

## v2.4.0
- Consistency over streaks: replaced streak counter with "X of 7 days tracked" display
- Food mood tracking: tap an emoji (energised/normal/sluggish/bloated) after logging a meal
- Training day toggle: boost calorie (+15%) and protein (+12%) targets on workout days
- CSV export: download all meal data as a spreadsheet from Settings
- Monthly report: 30-day averages, top foods, weight trend, mood patterns, and AI insight

## v2.3.0
- Gut Health onboarding step with focus toggle
- Gut Health Score (0-100) across 4 pillars: prebiotic, probiotic, anti-inflammatory, fiber diversity
- Gut score displayed on meal confirmation, timeline cards, calendar detail, and meal detail modal
- Fermented Foods and Prebiotic Foods categories in diet plan picker (conditional on gut health focus)
- Gut health context in AI prompts and diet plan generation
- Gut Health sub-tab in diet plan with fermented rotation, prebiotic suggestions, and fiber goals
- Dynamic diet plan sub-tabs based on user preferences

## v2.2.0
- Diet plan builder with food category picker and AI-generated meal plans
- Diet plan sub-tabs: Diet Plan, Grocery List, Supplements, Lifestyle
- Eating Patterns onboarding step (snacking, cravings, emotional eating)
- Supplements onboarding step with toggle
- Meal detail expansion (tap to see ingredients and macros)
- Today button on diet plan header
- MacAI branding for AI chat (renamed from NutriBites)
